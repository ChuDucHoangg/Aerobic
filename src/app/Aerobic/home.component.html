REASON

Regular Aerobic Exercise For Good Health

Aerobic exercise provides cardiovascular conditioning. The term aerobic actually means "with oxygen," which means that breathing controls how much oxygen can be delivered to the muscles to help them burn fuel and move.

A day you only need to spend 45 minutes doing aerobic exercise for moderate intensity exercisers. As for those who exercise with intense intensity, it only takes 30 minutes a day.

BENIFIT

What do you get when you do aerobics?

Weight Loss

Aerobic exercises are often practiced with music, music helps maintain speed, so it will optimize calories.

Supple

Aerobic exercise can make you feel tired at first. However, in the long run, you will feel more supple, more physically stable and less tired.

Mood

If you maintain a vigorous pace, your body will release endorphins, the body's natural pain relievers. This helps the aerobic exerciser's mood to be healthy and increase euphoria.

Reduce Disease

+ Prevent Osteoporosis.

+ Lower blood pressure and control blood sugar.

+ Better control of coronary artery disease.

+ Reducing the risk of diseases such as obesity.
